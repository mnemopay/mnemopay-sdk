## Pricing discipline

Non-negotiables. Deviation requires written reason in this file.

1. **No free tier** except the capped "first 10 MCP servers" promo. Everything else is paid from day 1.
2. **No discounts past 50%.** Buyers who pay more complain less.
3. **No indefinite free trials.** 30-day max, then card on file converts automatically.
4. **African buyers: no free tier at all.** Pricing signals seriousness; free signals frivolity.
5. **Minimum pilot commitment: 3 months.** Month-to-month only after the pilot ends cleanly.
6. **Success criteria written into every pilot contract.** "We'll know this worked if ___" — signed by both sides before money moves.
