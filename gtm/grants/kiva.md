## Why Kiva (not a grant, but functionally equivalent)

- 0% interest = free money to the business for 3 years
- No credit check — approval is based on "social underwriting": 5-25 friends/family lend small amounts first, unlocking the public campaign
- Works for any entity stage — pre-revenue through profitable
- Approval rate: ~80% of applications that complete the social-underwriting phase

## Loan request narrative (prepared for paste)

**Business:** J&B Enterprise LLC, d/b/a BizSuite

BizSuite is a bootstrapped AI toolkit for small businesses — 20 tools sold as a one-time purchase that replace an average of $35,000/year in SaaS subscriptions. Built and shipped by Jerry Omiagbo, a Black full-stack engineer in Dallas. Live at getbizsuite.com with paying customers on Stripe.

**How will this impact your community:**

If this loan unlocks 50 new customers, that is $140K/month of capital retained in small communities across the US — far more impact per dollar than a traditional grant.
