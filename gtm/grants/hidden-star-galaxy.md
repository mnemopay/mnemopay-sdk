## Business summary (2-3 paragraphs)

BizSuite is an all-in-one AI toolkit for small businesses and agencies — 20 tools across marketing, sales, content, and operations, sold as a one-time purchase at $997/$1,497/$2,497. The insight is that the average SMB pays $35,000/year for a patchwork of SaaS subscriptions (HubSpot, Jasper, Canva Pro, ClickUp, dozens more), and most of them can be replaced by a single locally-hosted toolkit powered by modern AI models.

Launched by Jerry Omiagbo in early 2026 under J&B Enterprise LLC (Texas), BizSuite is bootstrapped, shipping, and generating revenue through a direct-to-business sales motion. The site is live at getbizsuite.com, the Stripe checkout is live, and the product has replaced an average of 11 tools per customer in early deployments.

The mission is specifically aimed at minority-owned and small agency operators who cannot afford enterprise SaaS bundles. Every BizSuite customer keeps an average of $2,800/month in their own pocket instead of sending it to Silicon Valley.
