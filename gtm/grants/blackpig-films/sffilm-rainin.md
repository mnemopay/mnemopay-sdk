# SFFILM Rainin Grant

**Amount:** $25,000
**URL:** https://www.sffilm.org/filmmaker-programs/sffilm-rainin-grant/
**Deadline:** May 8 (narrative) / May 22 (documentary) — 2026 cycle
**Status:** DRAFT

---

## What it funds

Supports feature-length narrative and documentary films in development or production. One of the most respected regional film grants in the US. Judged on artistic merit, social relevance, and filmmaker vision.

---

## Eligibility

- US-based filmmakers
- Feature-length projects (narrative or documentary)
- Development, pre-production, or production stage
- Strong preference for films with social impact or underrepresented stories
- No prior SFFILM Rainin recipient (check if first-time applicant)

## Angle

SFFILM Rainin has historically funded films about underrepresented communities, social justice, and human rights. A Dallas-based Black filmmaker with a story rooted in Black experience, diaspora, urban life, or systemic issues is exactly the profile this grant was designed for. Lead with the story's social stakes in the synopsis and director's statement.
