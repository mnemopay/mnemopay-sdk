## Jerry Omiagbo — Writer / Director / Producer

Jerry Omiagbo is a Nigerian-American filmmaker and software engineer based in Dallas, TX. He is the founder of Blackpig Films, an independent production company focused on Nigerian-American and diaspora stories.

Jerry has spent the past several years building technical infrastructure for AI systems while developing his film practice in parallel. Eligible is his feature debut in development — a project rooted in his experience growing up Nigerian-American and watching the men around him navigate the cost of immigrant success.

As a filmmaker, Jerry works at the intersection of intimate character study and grounded social observation. He is drawn to stories about people who are very good at one version of themselves and have no idea who they are otherwise.
