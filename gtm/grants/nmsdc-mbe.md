# NMSDC MBE Certification — Application Prep

**Program:** National Minority Supplier Development Council (NMSDC) Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) Certification
**URL:** nmsdc.org
**Cost:** Varies by regional affiliate ($350-$1,500 depending on revenue tier); Reimbursement Initiative may cover first-year fee
**Award:** Certification unlocks access to the supplier-diversity spend of Fortune 500 corporations — $600B+/year in contracts earmarked for minority-owned suppliers
**Status:** BLOCKED — requires tax documents, bank statements, and formation documents

---

## Why this is higher-leverage than any single grant

Every Fortune 500 company has a supplier-diversity target (usually 5-10% of total procurement spend). To count toward that target, a supplier must be NMSDC-certified. Once J&B Enterprise LLC has MBE certification, BizSuite becomes eligible for corporate-procurement conversations that would otherwise never happen — corporate buyers actively *look* for certified minority vendors to hit their diversity targets.

**Example real-world pipeline unlocked:**
- BizSuite sold to corporate procurement teams at any F500 that needs to hit a 2026 supplier-diversity target (hundreds of companies)
- MnemoPay / GridStamp sold to tech-forward F500 subsidiaries that want to check the "minority-owned" box on their AI vendor procurement
- Even if only 3-5 F500 conversations open up, that is easily > $100K in annual contract value at BizSuite Enterprise tier ($299/mo) pricing

- [ ] **Proof of Dallas residence** (utility bill)

- [ ] **Website screenshots** for each d/b/a

## Certifying affiliate

For Dallas, the certifying NMSDC affiliate is the **Dallas/Fort Worth Minority Supplier Development Council** (dfwmsdc.org) — apply there, not through the national NMSDC portal directly.

## Reimbursement Initiative

Check dfwmsdc.org and nmsdc.org for a current "Reimbursement Initiative" that refunds the certification fee for first-time applicants — this was active in 2024-2025 and is worth confirming before paying out of pocket.
