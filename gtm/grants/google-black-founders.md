**Company:** J&B Enterprise LLC, d/b/a MnemoPay

**What MnemoPay does (one sentence):**
MnemoPay is the reputation and payments infrastructure for autonomous AI agents — a memory + payments + credit-score primitive that lets AI agents accumulate portable, verifiable trust as they transact.

**What MnemoPay has shipped (proof):**
- v1.0.1 live on npm as @mnemopay/sdk (TypeScript) and mnemopay (Python)

- 707 tests passing including a 92-day 5,500-agent stress simulation
- Multi-rail payments (Stripe, Lightning, Paystack, x402)
