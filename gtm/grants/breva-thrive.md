# Breva Thrive Grant — $5K Quarterly

## Eligibility
- For-profit entity, legally formed and in good standing in any U.S. state
- Principal place of business in the United States
- 12+ months operations (strongly preferred)
- Community impact focus

## Why J&B Enterprise LLC qualifies
- Texas LLC in good standing
- Building AI infrastructure (MnemoPay, GridStamp) that enables autonomous commerce for underserved markets
- Paystack integration specifically serves African fintech market (underserved)
- Open source, Apache 2.0 — community benefit

## Application notes
- Free to apply
- Must upload required documentation
- Funds must be used for business mission or community-impact goals
- Equipment, hiring, product development, targeted outreach are acceptable uses

## Pitch angle
MnemoPay is open-source payment infrastructure for AI agents, with specific emphasis on serving African markets through Paystack integration. GridStamp enables verifiable delivery for autonomous robots, reducing fraud in last-mile logistics. Both products are open source and serve communities that are typically underserved by enterprise payment infrastructure.
